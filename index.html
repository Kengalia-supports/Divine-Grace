About

How Caring for the Elderly Became My Dream

It all began when I was speaking with my uncle in Nigeria. He shared a story about an elderly woman from his neighborhood who had recently passed away. Her children, who lived abroad, had come to celebrate the life of their beloved mother, believing she had spent her final years in comfort. But what they didn't know was the suffering she endured in her last days. The family had hired a caregiver to look after her, but instead of providing the compassion and support she needed, the caregiver neglected her duty. Each morning, after preparing her breakfast, the caregiver would lock the elderly woman outside and leave for the day to attend to her own personal matters, only returning in the evening to make dinner. Left alone for hours, the woman would wander the streets aimlessly. The locals began to see her as confused, perhaps even mentally ill old woman. Hearing this broke my heart. i was deeply moved by the thought of this woman spending her final days in loneliness and neglect. That's when i knew i wanted to make a difference. i decided to open a facility where the elderly would be treated with love, respect, honor, and dignity- ensuring they find comfort and care in their later years, just as they deserve.
